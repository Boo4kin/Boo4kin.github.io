Shorts Farm Uploader - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

1. Overview

Shorts Farm Uploader is a private desktop tool used to prepare, manage, and upload short-form video content to TikTok accounts authorized by the user.

2. Data Processed

The app may process video files, titles, descriptions, publishing settings, TikTok authorization tokens, upload status information, and related local metadata needed for the upload workflow.

3. How Data Is Used

Data is used only to prepare, upload, schedule, and manage video publishing through TikTok APIs on behalf of the authenticated user.

4. Local Storage

Project files, queue information, and authorization tokens may be stored locally on the user's computer. Tokens are used to avoid asking the user to authorize the app again for every upload.

5. Sharing

The app does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. Video content and metadata are sent to TikTok only when the user starts an upload or publishing workflow.

6. Revoking Access

Users can revoke app access at any time through their TikTok account settings. After access is revoked, the app will no longer be able to publish videos through the revoked authorization.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact email listed in the TikTok Developer application associated with this service.
