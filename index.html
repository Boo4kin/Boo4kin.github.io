Responsible publishing

Creators stay in control

ClipFlow Studio is for creators, clubs, editors, and sports media teams publishing their own or licensed video content. The service does not publish automatically without creator review.

  • Explicit TikTok authorization through Login Kit
  • Per-video rights confirmation before publishing
  • Editable captions, hashtags, and visibility settings
  • Clear publishing status after API submission
