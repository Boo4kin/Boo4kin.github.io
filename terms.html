ClipFlow Studio Terms of Service

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. Purpose

ClipFlow Studio is a web application for sports creators, clubs, editors, and media teams who prepare short clips from footage they own or are licensed to use and publish selected clips to their own social accounts.

2. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring that every uploaded video, audio track, caption, title, hashtag, and related asset is owned by them or used with the necessary licenses and permissions.

3. Publishing Control

ClipFlow Studio does not publish a clip without user action. Users must review the clip preview, confirm rights, and choose to publish or upload each selected video.

4. TikTok Access

The service may request TikTok permissions such as publishing or video upload scopes. The authenticated TikTok user can grant or deny those permissions and can revoke access through TikTok account settings.

5. Compliance

Users must comply with TikTok's Terms of Service, Community Guidelines, copyright rules, platform policies, and all applicable laws when preparing or publishing content.

6. Service Availability

The service is provided as available. TikTok may reject, moderate, restrict, or remove content according to its own policies and account settings.

7. Contact

For questions, use the support email listed in the TikTok Developer profile for ClipFlow Studio.
